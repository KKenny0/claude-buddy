What it does

🔇

Silent by design

Hooks produce zero stdout. No additionalContext, no conversation injection. All feedback lives on the statusline and on-demand detail card.

🏃

Rhythm coach

Error avalanche alerts, file grinding detection, session fatigue warnings. Your buddy watches your coding patterns and surfaces signals when they matter.

📈

XP and leveling

20 levels with gated feature unlocks. Gain XP from commits, tool usage, streaks, and daily interactions. Hatch at Lv.1, unlock the statusline at Lv.3, evolve at Lv.15, prestige at Lv.20.

🥚

Deterministic generation

SHA-256 based species, rarity, and stats derived from your username. Same user always gets the same pet. Plus a 1% shiny chance.

📟

Native statusline

Always-visible mood, mode, streak, and coach signals right in Claude Code. Three presence modes: quiet, focus (default), and lively.

🖥️

tmux panel

Optional temporary popup or live sidebar watcher for terminal power users. Works outside Claude Code too.
